about places.map()

places.map() is an app in which you mark your favorite places on a map, and see the markers other users have placed.

places.map() uses the Mapbox API to display a map and to place markers, and uses Geocoder to convert your plain text input into latitude and longitude coordinates.


place.map() also has a search feature, which you can use to find the places you want to mark. it's helpful, considering the geocoder can sometimes return odd results, as it doesn't just include geographical locations, but businesses as well. best to check in the search bar before adding on the form, just to make sure.



there is also a directions feature, which you can use to find the route between two places. click on a step to zoom in and see it up close.
